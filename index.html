About Me

Hi, I'm Pankaj Kushwaha — a WordPress Full-Stack Developer based in Satna, Madhya Pradesh. I specialize in turning design mockups into high-performance, maintainable WordPress websites.

With 2+ years of hands-on experience, I've built everything from medical professional websites and furniture e-commerce stores to LMS platforms and company portfolio sites. I'm equally comfortable working with raw PHP/HTML/CSS as I am with Elementor or WooCommerce customization.

Before specializing in WordPress, I gained solid backend fundamentals through Java full-stack development with Spring Boot and J2EE — which makes my WordPress code cleaner and more structured than most.
